MAIN CHARACTER

Hodaka Morishima

A first year high school student who ran away from home and moved to Tokyo. In his teen years, he seemed to be going through a rebellious phase and had a strained relationship with his parents, particularly his father. It is implied he is abused and controlled by his father, and combined with his school life, was made to feel "chained down."

Hina Amano

A third year junior high school student who is taking various part-time jobs to support her family after her mother's death. She has a very unique ability to control the weather with various forms such as the rain and lightning with her emotions while bringing sunlight by praying.

OTHER CHARACTER

Nagisa Amano

Nicknamed "Nagi", an elementary student. He is Hina Amano's younger brother lives with her.


Keisuke Suga

A middle-aged man who runs a small publishing company where Hodaka ends up working part-time.


Natsumi Suga

Keisuke's niece and a college student who works part-time at his office.


Yasui

An old police officer working with Takai, who is working on Hodaka's case.


Takai

A police officer in the Tokyo force and Yasui's partner, who is working on Hodaka's case.


Fumi Tachibana

Taki's grandmother and one of the customers of Hodaka and Hina's sunshine girl service.


Taki Tachibana

A character from Your Name and Fumi's grandson.


Mitsuha Miyamizu

A character from Your Name, who works as a jewelry store saleswoman in Shinjuku.
